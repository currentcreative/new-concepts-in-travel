About Us

New Concepts Travel was founded in 1995 by Veronica Jimenez, an experienced travel professional who's been in the industry for more than 20 years.

New Concepts Travel is dedicated to giving clients the insight and service they need to maximize corporate travel savings, plan meetings and conferences, and enjoy spectacular vacations. We have first-hand knowledge of the world's top destinations, and we can customize a program or itinerary to suit all tastes and budgets. Our experienced travel professionals will coordinate every aspect of each trip, and they're available 24 hours a day.

New Concepts Travel is an affiliate of the Tzell Travel group which is ranked as the Number #1 Travel company by BTN (Business Travel News) this year.

About Veronica Jimenez

For New Concepts Travel founder and President Veronica Jimenez, travel is a family business. She began her career in the airline industry, working at a major carrier for 6 years before taking a job at one of New York's leading travel agencies. She was eventually promoted to a senior management position, in charge of both wholesale and retail travel. In 1995, Veronica left to establish New Concepts Travel and bring her expertise to a broad range of corporate and individual clients.
